ABOUT BOOKS

Books are a medium for recording information through writing or images. They are a source of knowledge and learning, and have been a part of our lives since ancient times. Books can be fiction or non-fiction, and can be sold in stores, online, or borrowed from libraries.


Here are some things to know about books: History The earliest writings may have been carved on beech wood, and the Latin word codex originally meant "block of wood". Types Books can be handwritten, printed on pages, or digitized online. Importance Books are important for education, and can help children develop their vocabulary and self-study skills. Benefits Books can help us learn about history, science, the arts, and more. They can also help us understand how these things affect us and our world. Accessibility The book industry has seen a rise in formats designed for greater accessibility, such as braille printing and large-print editions.
