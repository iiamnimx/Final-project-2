Books

How books are beneficial?

Books can improve concentration and reading skills, and they can help build knowledge of the world. They can also uplift mood and enhance vocabulary.

What books are?

Books are a medium for recording information in the form of writing or images. They are usually made up of many pages bound together and protected by a cover. How books have evolved.

What books are used for?

Books are used for telling stories, archiving history, and sharing information. They can be fiction, which contains invented content, or non-fiction, which contains content intended as factual truth.
